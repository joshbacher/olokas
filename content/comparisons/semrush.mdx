Semrush is a mature, broad SEO platform. It does keyword research, rank tracking, backlinks, site audits, content tooling, and a long list of adjacent features. That breadth is the product. If you want one tool that covers most of your search-marketing surface, Semrush is a defensible choice.

Olokas is not that. Olokas only watches one thing: how four AI engines — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and Claude — answer questions about your business. That is the entire feature set. We are not trying to replace Semrush, and we don't recommend running both unless your team has both budgets.

This page is a stub. Real side-by-side detail will be added by a human who has used both tools recently — until then, the table above is the most accurate honest summary we have.

## When to choose Olokas

- Your team is small, your budget is tight, and the question you actually need answered is "are we showing up in AI answers."
- You already have an SEO tool you like and you want a focused complement that monitors AI surfaces specifically.
- You don't need keyword research or backlink data, and a $39/mo line item that does one thing well is a better fit than a $140/mo suite.
- You want a weekly email that summarizes per-engine GEO scores and flags movement, not a dashboard you have to log into.

## When to choose Semrush

- You are running a real SEO program and need keyword research, rank tracking, and a backlink database in one place.
- You have a team that already lives inside Semrush and the workflow tax of switching tools is high.
- AI search visibility is a real concern but not your top concern, and Semrush's AI Overview tracking inside its keyword reports is enough for now.
- You want a single vendor for most search-related questions and are willing to pay enterprise-suite pricing for that consolidation.

## Bottom line

Semrush is a suite. Olokas is a tool. The two products solve overlapping problems with very different scopes. Most teams will pick the one that matches their actual workflow rather than running both — and we'd rather you pick Semrush than buy Olokas under the wrong assumption.
