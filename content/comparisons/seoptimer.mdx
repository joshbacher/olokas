SEOptimer focuses on quick, broad-strokes on-page SEO audits — the kind agencies use to generate a leave-behind for a prospect. It's good at producing a one-page report that says "here are the obvious things to fix on this site." It also offers an embeddable form so agencies can capture leads.

Olokas is built for an ongoing question, not a one-shot audit: how is your business showing up in AI answers each week, across the four engines that matter, and what's changing. Different timescale, different surface.

This page is a stub. A human will replace it with a deeper comparison once we've used both tools alongside each other. For now the table above is our honest summary.

## When to choose Olokas

- Your concern is AI answer-engine visibility, not on-page SEO hygiene.
- You want a recurring weekly signal, not a one-time audit you have to remember to re-run.
- You already have a basic SEO audit tool or a developer who keeps the on-page basics in shape.
- You like the agency-friendly white-label option for client-facing weekly reports.

## When to choose SEOptimer

- You need a fast, broad on-page audit you can hand to a client today.
- You want an embeddable lead-gen audit widget on your agency site.
- Your bottleneck is producing client-facing audit deliverables at volume.
- You don't need ongoing AI answer monitoring — a periodic check is enough for your workflow.

## Bottom line

SEOptimer is a great agency-prospecting and on-page audit tool. Olokas is a focused weekly monitor for AI search visibility. Most agencies that need both will buy both — together they cost less than most all-in-one suites and they don't overlap.
