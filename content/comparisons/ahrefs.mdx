Ahrefs is best known for the depth of its backlink index and the polish of its rank-tracking and keyword-research workflows. If you have heard "Ahrefs says…" cited in an SEO meeting, that's why. The product is broad and well-instrumented for traditional SEO work.

Olokas does not try to compete with that. Olokas runs target queries against ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and Claude every week, records whether your domain shows up, and emails you the result. That's the scope.

This page is a stub. A human-edited comparison from someone using both tools day to day will replace this — for now the table above is our honest summary.

## When to choose Olokas

- You want a cheap, focused way to know whether AI answer engines are citing your business.
- You don't need backlink data or keyword research and don't want to pay for them as part of a bundle.
- You prefer a single weekly email over a dashboard, and a clear per-engine GEO score over a forest of metrics.
- You suspect AI search is taking traffic share from classic SERPs and want a number to track over time.

## When to choose Ahrefs

- You're doing serious SEO and the backlink index is the reason you're picking a tool.
- Your team needs keyword research, content gap analysis, and rank tracking in one place.
- You're tracking a competitive landscape where link-building decisions actually move the dial, and link data quality matters.
- You can absorb the price and you'll use enough of the suite to justify it.

## Bottom line

Ahrefs is the right tool for backlink-driven SEO. Olokas is the right tool for AI-engine visibility. They are not interchangeable, and we'd rather you keep paying Ahrefs than churn it for us — Olokas works fine alongside an existing SEO stack as a focused add-on.
