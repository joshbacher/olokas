## 5-section outline

1. **What AI Overviews actually are, and why they changed the traffic math** — AI Overviews now appear in ~60% of U.S. queries (up from 31% a year ago), with coverage highest in B2B tech (82%), education (83%), and healthcare (88%). Position #1 no longer guarantees traffic: the AI Overview sits above it. The goal has shifted from "rank first" to "get cited inside the answer." This section sets the stakes and clears up the common confusion between AI Mode, AI Overviews, and standard organic results.

2. **The content signals that drive citations** — Google's AI extracts discrete, self-contained claims. Pages with semantically complete answers in 134–167-word self-contained units are 4.2× more likely to be cited than pages that bury answers in narrative. Question-based queries trigger AI Overviews 84% of the time, up from 60% in early 2025. This section walks through exactly what "semantically complete" means with before/after content examples.

3. **Schema and entity signals: how small businesses compete without big domain authority** — 47% of AI Overview citations come from pages ranked below position #5 in traditional results — domain authority alone doesn't win citations. FAQ schema, organization schema, and consistent entity signals (author bios, named brand across properties) are the equalizers. This section is practical and narrow: exactly which schema types to implement, with no-setup-cost tools to do it.

4. **The measurement problem: why you probably don't know if you're being cited** — Standard rank trackers (even premium ones) don't instrument AI Overview citations. A page can be cited in an AI Overview without ranking in the top 10 for that query, and a page ranking #1 may not appear in the overview at all. This section explains what to measure (citation presence, citation frequency by query, delta vs. organic click share) and what categories of tool track it — without being a product ad.

5. **A practical weekly action plan for SMBs** — Condensed, sequenced. Week 1: audit three priority queries for AI Overview presence manually. Week 2: update the top-three pages with self-contained answer blocks and FAQ schema. Week 3: confirm entity consistency across Google Business Profile, About page, and LinkedIn. Week 4: set up citation monitoring so you stop guessing. Realistic for an owner spending 3–4 hours on SEO per week.
